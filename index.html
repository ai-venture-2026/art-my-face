About Us

Where Art Meets Celebration

Art My Face is a professional event entertainment company specializing in face painting, balloon twisting, glitter tattoos, temporary tattoos, and creative party experiences for all ages.

Led by Cynthia Rosario, our team brings color, joy, and unforgettable artistry to every event we serve. Whether it's a child's birthday party, a corporate team event, a school carnival, or a community festival — we transform ordinary gatherings into vibrant, creative celebrations.

We use only professional-grade, skin-safe, non-toxic paints and materials, so every guest can enjoy the fun with complete peace of mind. From whimsical butterflies and fierce superheroes to elegant glitter designs, our artists craft custom creations that make every face a masterpiece.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do your services cost?

Pricing depends on the number of guests, event duration, and services requested. Face painting typically starts around a base rate and increases with event size. Contact us for a free custom quote.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and holidays. Popular dates fill up quickly, so the sooner you reach out, the better.

Are the paints safe for skin?

Absolutely. We use only professional, skin-safe, non-toxic face paints designed specifically for body art. Your safety and comfort is our top priority.

Is face painting only for kids?

Not at all! Face painting is fun for all ages. We create designs suitable for kids, teens, and adults — from playful characters to elegant festival looks.

How long does each face painting take?

Most designs take 3–5 minutes per guest, depending on complexity. This means we can serve a large number of guests efficiently at your event.

Can I request a specific theme?

Yes! We love themed events. We can customize designs to match your party theme — superheroes, princesses, Halloween, carnival, and more.

How many guests can you accommodate?

The number of guests depends on event duration and number of artists. We can arrange additional entertainers for larger events to keep lines short and fun flowing.

What do you need from the host?

Just a small table, two chairs, and a shaded or indoor area for the artist to work comfortably. We bring everything else.

What if it rains during an outdoor event?

We recommend having a covered or indoor backup space available. As long as we have a dry area to work, the show goes on!

Where do you provide services?

Art My Face serves events across New Jersey, New York City, and nearby areas. Contact us to confirm availability in your area.
